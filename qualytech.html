QualyTech

This individual school project marked my first experience building a web application using Laravel PHP. Over the course of a semester, as part of the Web Development course, I developed a functional e-commerce platform focused on high-quality monitors and graphics cards. The webshop was designed to highlight premium technology products, offering a streamlined user experience with a clear emphasis on performance and quality.

What I Learned

During this project, I gained valuable experience in web design, particularly in creating more effective and visually appealing layouts. It also introduced me to Laravel PHP, which I found to be a powerful and intuitive framework that made backend development significantly more efficient.

I especially enjoyed working with Alpine.js and Tailwind CSS, tools that allowed me to bring my designs to life quickly and responsively. This project not only strengthened my technical skills but also reinforced my passion for creative web development. Web design continues to be one of my favorite areas in IT, as it gives me the freedom to express my ideas and creativity through clean, user-focused interfaces.

My Role in the Project

This was an individual project, meaning all of the code and functionalities were developed solely by me. While the course provided several examples and reference materials, the final implementation reflects my own understanding of the concepts. It demonstrates my ability to apply PHP, Laravel, Alpine.js, and Tailwind CSS creatively and independently in a real-world context.
