De Beerse Bende

This project was completed as part of the Skill Integration 2 course, where I worked closely with a team of classmates to define and design a solution for a simulated client — one of our lecturers who assigned us the task. The goal was to replace the manual process of organizing and communicating about a stage performance. Instead of relying on handwritten notes or phone calls, the client wanted a web application where clients could easily access and manage relevant information.

As a team, we created a detailed use case diagram and wrote descriptions for each required functionality based on the client’s expectations. We concluded the project with a non-functional demo design, visually presenting the layout, features and must have functionalities of the web application.

What I Learned

During this project, I gained valuable experience in team collaboration and learned how to identify the most effective ways to turn a client’s idea into a practical digital solution. We created a detailed use case diagram with accompanying descriptions to map out the required functionalities, using StarUML for visual modeling.

To bring the concept to life visually, we used Figma to design a mockup of the web application. This allowed us to present a clear demo to the client, showcasing how each feature would work and gathering valuable feedback to refine our ideas.

Additionally, I deepened my understanding of Jira and how to use it efficiently within a sprint-based workflow. Managing tasks, assigning responsibilities, and tracking progress through different development phases gave me a solid foundation in project planning and agile methodology.

My Role in the Project

I worked in a team of 6 students, where we collaborated closely using Jira to organize our tasks, manage communication, and ensure an even distribution of work throughout the project.

My main responsibility was creating the use case diagram using StarUML, with continuous input and feedback from the team to refine the final structure. Additionally, I worked extensively in Figma, where I created the main demo design of the application and developed functionality mockups to demonstrate how each feature would work. This visual demo played a key role in presenting our concept to the client and gathering valuable feedback.

Team members: Bram van Asperen - Daan Borghs - Senne Fabri - Tijl Cleynhens - Siyar Yildirim
