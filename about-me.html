About Me

Introduction

Hello! I’m Jaro Peeters, a second-year student in Application Development and Artificial Intelligence at Thomas More Kempen in Geel, Belgium. I was born in Lommel, Limburg on January 26, 2005, and currently live in Pelt. My passion for technology started at a young age and has only grown stronger over the years.

I’m especially interested in exploring both software and hardware developments — I love staying up to date with the latest tech and experimenting with new tools. In my free time, I focus on expanding my knowledge through self-learning and hands-on projects. I also enjoy working on 3D modeling, which may be a direction I’ll explore further in the future.

Beyond technology, I’m passionate about sports, fitness, and learning about nutrition. I value time with my family and friends, and I always try to create a positive environment wherever I go. I’m a motivated learner who’s always striving to grow — both technically and personally.

Why I chose for Application Development

I completed my secondary education with a focus on Technical Sciences, a program that emphasizes scientific principles and analytical thinking. While I’ve always had an interest in science, my true passion has consistently been technology. From a young age, I’ve been highly motivated to explore the latest innovations and continuously expand my understanding of the digital world.

What drives me most is the desire to make a difference — to create something meaningful that helps others while also building valuable experience and a strong professional network. That’s why I chose Application Development: it combines my love for building digital solutions with the opportunity to grow within a dynamic company environment.

I find great satisfaction in creating something from the ground up and delivering results that make a client truly happy. This is what motivates me — constantly learning about emerging technologies, collaborating in a team, and working towards solutions that bring real value to users and clients alike.

My Dreams and Ambitions

My future ambitions and dreams are rooted in the desire to make a positive impact on the world. I aspire to create things that not only bring joy to people, but also help them connect with one another. There’s nothing more fulfilling to me than seeing others smile because of something I helped build — it reminds me that the time and energy I invest can be meaningful and purpose-driven.

One of my biggest ambitions is to one day start my own company or business, where I can work alongside a dedicated team to develop solutions that have a positive and beautiful impact on society. I believe in the power of innovation, collaboration, and using technology to create something that truly matters.
